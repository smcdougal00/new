About Me

Getting to know Spencer

Hello! My name is Spencer McDougal. I am the second oldest of seven children and am a Sophmore at BYU. I love playing sports, traveling, and spending time with family and friends. I hope you enjoy learning a little bit about me!

I was born in Salt Lake City, Utah in 2000. From a young age, I have always been interested by business. Through much trial and error, I finally decided upon pursuing a Career in Finance. I apply for the program this summer and am excited to see where this path will take me!

I served an LDS mission in Cote D'Ivoire from 2018-2019. I loved my mission and the many lessons I learned through it. One skill I was able to develop was the ability to speak French. Thanks to this skill, I have grown to love the French Culture and the popele in France as well. I hope to live in Paris one day!
